Use valid sizes listed above.- **AI Video timeout:** Increase `--max-polls` to 120 or `--poll-interval` to 10.- **Programmatic too slow:** Reduce resolution to 180x320, pre-compute all blur masks, use cv2.VideoWriter instead of PNG saving.- **Video won't play in browser:** Re-encode with H.264: `ffmpeg -y -i input.mp4 -c:v libx264 -crf 20 -pix_fmt yuv420p output.mp4`- **Gaussian blur too slow:** Use `cv2.GaussianBlur` (OpenCV) instead of `scipy.ndimage.gaussian_filter` — it's 2-5x faster.- **Frame saving too slow:** Use `cv2.VideoWriter` directly instead of saving individual PNGs then encoding with ffmpeg.
